About Us

King City
Medical Pharmacy

King City Medical Pharmacy is a locally owned independent pharmacy. What sets us apart is our dedication to customer service. We take our responsibilities of providing high quality prescription medications with accuracy, very seriously.

Conveniently located in a major healthcare establishment, we provide free delivery service to all of Ontario and continue to live up to our motto: 'the pharmacy you can trust'.

Our Mission

Help customers get medicines at the lowest cost while maintaining the highest accuracy and quality.

Our Vision

To remain the pharmacy you can trust — through compassionate, consistent, community-centred care.

Accuracy

Every prescription handled with precision and the highest safety standards.

Compassion

We treat every patient as a person — with empathy, patience, and genuine care.

Empowerment

We educate patients on their medications, options, and best approaches.

Trust

Earned through transparency, consistency, and putting your needs first.

Affordability

We work hard to ensure you receive medicines at the lowest possible cost.

Community

As a locally owned pharmacy, we are invested in King City's long-term health.
